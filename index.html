🌊 Baby Moses River Adventure 🚼
Long ago in Egypt, baby Moses was placed in a basket to float on the river Nile in order to protect his life from Pharaoh's cruel law. 🎯 Your mission is to guide him safely through the treacherous waters so he can reach Pharaoh's daughter safely!

🎮 How to Play

1.Scan the QR code above with your phone or tablet camera.
2.Enter the access code MOSES2026 when prompted.
3.Choose a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
4.Use the on-screen arrow buttons to guide Baby Moses through the river.
5.Visit all 10 golden star checkpoints and answer Bible questions.
6.Avoid river creatures (crocodiles, sharks, octopus) and bombs!
7.Reach Pharaoh's daughter at the FINISH to complete your adventure!
⭐ Scoring: +10 pts per correct answer | -5 pts per clue used | Max score: 100 pts!

Tips for Success

  • Don't know the answer? Tap "Show Clue" — each clue guides you closer to the answer!
  • Learning is part of the adventure — it's okay to use clues to discover new Bible facts.
  • Watch out for river creatures! They pop up as obstacles along the maze path.
  • Try all three difficulty levels — Easy has fewer questions, Hard is the ultimate challenge!
