🌊 Baby Moses River Adventure 🚼
Long ago in Egypt, baby Moses was placed in a basket to float on the river Nile in order to protect his life from Pharaoh's cruel law. 🎯 Your mission is to guide him safely through the treacherous waters so he can reach Pharaoh's daughter safely!

🎮 How to Play

⌨️Use arrow buttons (or keyboard arrows) to move baby Moses from START to FINISH.
🌊Follow the blue water path — it's the only safe route for Moses!
⭐Visit all 10 checkpoints in order and answer Bible questions correctly.
💡Use clue buttons if you need help — each clue costs 5 points.
🚫You must answer each question to continue — no skipping!
Catch the pink gems! They drop in near Moses — grab each for +5 bonus points to boost your score, earn more ⭐ stars, and beat your best!
🛡️Grab a shield bubble to save Moses from one creature bump — no life lost!
⛈️Dodge the storm clouds! If Moses catches one, it washes away 5 points — so move around them.

⚠️ What Happens If You Go Off the Safe Path?

If Moses bumps into scary river creatures like crocodiles 🐊, sharks 🦈, or bombs 💣, he loses a life ❤️ and goes back to START — but you keep your checkpoints and score, so just keep going! You have 3 lives. Lose all three and the adventure starts over. 💪

⭐ Scoring: +10 pts per correct answer, +5 pts per pink gem , -5 pts per clue, and -5 pts if you catch a storm cloud ⛈️. More points = more stars!
⚠️ Please choose a difficulty level first!

📖 How to Play

🎯Mission: Guide baby Moses through the Nile River to reach Pharaoh's daughter at FINISH!
⌨️Controls: Arrow buttons or keyboard keys (↑ ↓ ← →) move Moses one step at a time.
🌊Safe Path: Stay on blue water cells — the only safe route!
⚠️Dangers: Brown cells with creatures and 💣 bombs cost Moses a life ❤️ and send him back to START — but you keep your checkpoints and score. You have 3 lives!
⭐Checkpoints: Visit all 10 gold stars IN ORDER and answer Bible questions correctly.
❓Questions: Try as many times as needed. Answer correctly to unlock each checkpoint!
💡Clues: Each clue costs 5 points — use them wisely!
Gems: Pink gems drop in near Moses — catch them for +5 points each! More points means more ⭐ stars and a higher best score.
🛡️Shields: Grab a shield bubble to block one creature/bomb hit — Moses keeps his life!
⛈️Storm clouds: Dodge these! Catching one washes away 5 points.
🏆Winning: Reach FINISH after ALL 10 checkpoints are done!
⭐Score: +10 per answer, +5 per gem, -5 per clue, -5 per storm cloud.
